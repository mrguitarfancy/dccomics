Superman is more than just a fictional comic book character, but a cultural icon that’s become ingrained into the public’s consciousness. He’s gone beyond being just a beloved comic book character, but an influential figure that’s helped shape a great deal of modern media and cultural traditions across the world. He not only helped build the superhero as a force in popular culture but became a symbol of the American Dream. Whether you like him or not, a lot is owed to the Man of Steel and his enduring presence in the media which will continue to have an impact for probably many more decades to come.
